The Voice Of God

Anirudh Iyer.


        The voice of God,
        Cajoles in times of need,
	The belief in God,
	Always pays heed.

	The medium is religion,
	Comforting while in fear, 
	Though it is a personal decision,
	Yet its influence does leer.

	To all, God appears as hope,
	By our side helping us cope.
